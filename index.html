Emphasizing Interpersonal Connection in Seminar-Style Blended Learning

Derek Thurber and Alyssa Dyar

Research Question

How can the Higher Education Administration program at Northwestern University maintain or strengthen the high quality, interpersonal learning experiences of graduate level seminar courses when offering them in a blended format?
